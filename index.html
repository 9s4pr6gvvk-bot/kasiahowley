01About Kasia

A coach shaped by
the stage and the science.

Kasia is a vocal coach and professional piano vocalist with over 7 years' coaching experience at leading drama schools, universities and conservatoires. She runs her private practice at Tileyard North, Wakefield, holds a BA (Hons) in Musical Theatre from ArtsEd London, and is completing a Master's in Vocal Pedagogy with the Voice Study Centre — turning the science of vocal fatigue into practical, everyday guidance.

"My approach is functional: efficient, sustainable technique that holds up under a strenuous schedule and a demanding stage."

Mentored by one of the North's leading Estill Master Trainers and a founding figure of CVT, she's also a qualified Vocal Health First Aider and Certified Vocal Massage Therapist — training that shapes her coaching, not a separate service. As a performer she's appeared as a soloist at the Royal Albert Hall and Trafalgar Square, and recorded alongside artists including Kerry Ellis and Ramin Karimloo. She prides herself on a safe, supportive coaching environment — her students now study and perform at leading drama schools and in the West End.

02Experience

From soloist to specialist.

Royal Albert Hall Trafalgar Square London Hippodrome Casino The Southbank Centre London O2 Indigo Arena

Private Practice & Teaching

Runs a private vocal coaching practice at Tileyard North, Wakefield, and is one of the technical voice tutors on the degree course at Emil Dale Academy, alongside teaching at universities and arts colleges across the UK.

Student & Client Outcomes

Students have secured places at leading drama schools including ArtsEd, Mountview, GSA, EDA and Urdang. Clients can be seen performing in West End, regional and international productions.

Working Musicians

Beyond musical theatre, Kasia works with professional musicians preparing for recording sessions, tours and session work — with a focus on sustainable voice use and longevity.

Notable Collaborations

Has performed alongside Kerry Ellis, Scott Alan, Oliver Tompsett and Dan & Laura Curtis, and features as a solo artist on a concept album alongside Ramin Karimloo, Lea Salonga and John Owen Jones.

Research-informed practice

03Vocal Coaching

Lessons built around your voice, not a method.

Whether you're training for drama school auditions, maintaining your voice through a long-running show, or preparing for a tour, sessions are tailored to your repertoire, your schedule and the demands placed on your voice.

One-to-one lessons are available in-person at Tileyard North, Wakefield, and online — for students, working performers and professional musicians alike.
