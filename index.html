01About Kasia

A coach shaped by
the stage and the science.

Kasia is a vocal coach with extensive experience running a private practice and training performers at some of the UK's leading drama schools. She holds a BA (Hons) in Musical Theatre from ArtsEd London, and is currently completing a Master's in Vocal Pedagogy with the Voice Study Centre — researching vocal fatigue and translating its science into practical, everyday guidance for working voices.

"My approach is functional: efficient, sustainable technique that holds up under a strenuous schedule and a demanding stage."

She has worked extensively with the Estill method under the mentorship of Andy Follin, and trained in myofascial release under Robert Price at the Voice Care Centre. Having performed as a soloist at the Royal Albert Hall, Trafalgar Square and the London O2 Indigo Arena, Kasia brings the realities of performance into every lesson she teaches.

04Vocal Coaching

Lessons built around your voice, not a method.

Whether you're training for drama school auditions, maintaining your voice through a long-running show, or preparing for a tour, sessions are tailored to your repertoire, your schedule and the demands placed on your voice.

One-to-one lessons are available in-person at Tileyard North, Leeds, and online — for students, working performers and professional musicians alike.
